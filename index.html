A few words

Muse are an English rock band, formed in 1994. The band consists of:

  • Matt Bellamy: vocals, guitar, keyboards
  • Chris Wolstenholme: bass guitar
  • Dominic Howard: drums

The band has been described as alternative rock, progressive rock and space rock.
